All Things Green

Purpose

The purpose of the All Things Green website is to provide a comprehensive, user-friendly database of trees, plants, shrubs, and flowers suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments—whether potted on a balcony or patio, or planted directly in a yard or garden. This platform is designed to serve as a resource for homeowners, gardeners, and landscaping enthusiasts of all experience levels. The site offers detailed, and accurate information on plant characteristics, and care requirements. It is designed to help gardeners and landscapers make informed plant choices based on climate, soil type, sunlight exposure, and maintenance preferences.

Audience

The All Things Green website is tailored for homeowners, gardeners, and outdoor enthusiasts of all experience levels—from curious beginners to seasoned landscapers. Whether cultivating a cozy balcony garden or planning a large landscape project, users can explore plant data and care guides customized to their needs, environments, and preferences.
